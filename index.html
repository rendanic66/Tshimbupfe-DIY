About Tshimbupfe DIY

We are a community-based organization dedicated to improving the quality of life in Tshimbupfe village through collaborative DIY projects.

Our Mission

To mobilize community resources and skills to address local challenges through sustainable DIY projects.

Our Vision

A self-reliant Tshimbupfe village where community members work together to create the infrastructure they need.

Our Values

Collaboration, sustainability, empowerment, and respect for local knowledge and traditions.



Our Story

Tshimbupfe DIY was founded in 2020 by a group of concerned village residents who recognized that by pooling their skills and resources, they could address many of the infrastructure challenges facing their community.

Starting with small projects like repairing the community hall and building a playground for children, the initiative quickly gained momentum as more villagers joined the effort.

Today, Tshimbupfe DIY coordinates multiple projects throughout the year, bringing together volunteers with diverse skills to improve the quality of life for all village residents.
